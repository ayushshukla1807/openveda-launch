# OpenVeda Roadmap 2026

Our mission is to build the world's most effective launchpad for open-source contributors. This roadmap outlines our strategic goals for 2026.

## Q1: Foundation & Expansion (Completed)
- [x] **Premium UI/UX Overhaul**: 100% human-authored, dual-theme design system.
- [x] **Multi-Program Support**: Integration with GSoC, LFX, Outreachy, and ESOC 2026.
- [x] **Platinum Playbooks**: High-signal contributor guides for top 20 organizations.
- [x] **Mentorship Hub 1.0**: Dynamic mentor profiles and 1-on-1 connection system.

## Q2: Community & Connectivity (In Progress)
- [ ] **OpenVeda Community Forum**: A dedicated space for contributors to share insights.
- [ ] **Live Workshop Series**: Weekly sessions with expert mentors on specific tech stacks.
- [ ] **Contributor Matchmaking**: AI-assisted (but human-filtered) project recommendations based on skill set.
- [ ] **Newsletter V2**: Automated weekly summaries of new "Good First Issues" across the ecosystem.

## Q3: Scaling & Global Outreach
- [ ] **University Partnerships**: Collaborating with top engineering colleges in India to drive open-source awareness.
- [ ] **OpenVeda Summit**: An annual virtual conference featuring alumni and program representatives.
- [ ] **Grant Program**: Financial support for exceptional contributors working on high-impact projects.

## Q4: OpenVeda Ecosystem
- [ ] **Project Incubation**: Helping community-led projects apply for and succeed in mentorship programs.
- [ ] **Advanced Analytics**: Detailed tracking of contributor progress and impact over time.
- [ ] **The "Contributor Portfolio"**: A verifiable, high-impact profile for users to showcase their work to employers.
